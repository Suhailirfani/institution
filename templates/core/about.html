ADABIYYA MODERN ACADEMY (AMAECT)

"Empowering Minds, Enriching Souls, Serving Humanity."

Our Story

Established on January 29, 2026, in Elad, Malappuram, the Adabiyya Modern Academy Educational Charitable Trust (AMAECT) is a global visionary movement dedicated to blending profound spiritual wisdom with cutting-edge modern education.

Under the visionary leadership of our Chairman, ABOOBACKER Shamil Irfani Elad, the Trust operates with a highly structured and transparent administrative framework. Our activities are managed by dedicated departments—including Higher Education, Shari’ah Studies, and Technology—ensuring professional excellence in every endeavor.

Our Vision

To be a premier global center of excellence that nurtures a generation of intellectually competent and ethically grounded individuals. Rooted in the noble ideology of Ahlu Sunnah Wal Jama'ah, we strive to bridge the gap between sacred tradition and modern innovation.

Our Mission & Objectives

Integrated Education

Establishing world-class institutions ranging from Dawa and Hifz colleges to Law, Arts, and Science academies.

Digital Transformation

Leveraging modern technology to provide accessible online educational platforms for learners worldwide.

Social Welfare

Uplifting the underprivileged through interest-free loans, medical aid, marriage assistance, and rural community adoption.

National Integrity

Upholding the values of the Indian Constitution and fostering a spirit of patriotism and social harmony.

Why Adabiyya?

At AMAECT, we don’t just teach; we transform. We believe that true education is a tool for social change and personal spiritual growth.
